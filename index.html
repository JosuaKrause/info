Josua (Joschi) Krause

Hi, I'm interested in the intersection of Visual Analytics and Machine Learning especially in the field of Finance and Health Care Analytics. I received my PhD in Computer Science from NYU Tandon School of Engineering, Brooklyn, NY under Prof. Dr. Enrico Bertini. Currently I'm working at Accern as VP of Data Science.
